About theretreat

No blogging experience? No worries!

The Blonde Abroad and The Institute of Code (IOC) are excited to announce an incredible all-inclusive blog mentorship retreat in Bali! Have a blog but looking to grow your audience and social media following? Learn the fundamentals of wordpress blogging and social media strategy so that you can gain the skills and confidence to grow.

Based on a short questionnaire evaluating your experience and goals, we will be personally inviting select applicants to one of our 10-day retreats lead by Kiersten of The Blonde Abroad and The Institute of Code. As an award-winning travel blogger, Kiersten will be sharing her expertise in SEO, brand development, brand partnerships, social media strategy, business development and web optimization. We'll also dive into editing programs like Lightroom and Photoshop to better your photography and graphic design skills. She will be joined by 6 other mentors with expertise in content strategy, photography, coding, and design.

The difference between our blog mentorship retreat and other blog courses is our team’s comprehensive approach to your education. Working one-on-one daily with a team of experts will ensure you have a deep understanding of blogging, a clear strategy on how to effectively tell your story and a plan on how to better connect with your audience.
