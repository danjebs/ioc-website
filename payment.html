Payment Options

Once you enroll you have the option to divide your remaining balance into installments up until 6-8 weeks before your retreat commencement date. You will be contacted shortly after enrollment to discuss the best options for you.

We accept bank transfers - please check with your bank for associated fees - and credit card - 2% transaction fee.

Scholarships

We currently offer $150 scholarships for anyone working for a non-profit organisation who's training will directly impact that business. We also offer scholarships to individuals suffering from considerable personal hardship - these range from $100 to $500 and are evaluated on an individual basis.

At various times we release additional scholarships which will be shared on social media and to those who have registered on our email list.

Pay Nothing Up Front

We currently have additional financial support options for US and Australian residents. For all other countries, please reach out and ask!

Australian Residents

Australian residents may be eligible to 'Study Now, Pay Later' through zipMoney. Zip Money provides our eligible students with the abliity to pay no up front course fees and no interest for 12 months. It only takes 3 minutes to check if you are eligible and checking your eligibility is not an obligation to commit to the course. You can

check your eligibility almost instantly here

Our agreement with ZipMoney includes no interest for 12 months , with the only eligible fees during that time a $75 account setup fee, and a $4.95 montlhy account keeping fee.

US Residents

We now have the ability for eligible US residents to pay nothing up front through low interest loans by our partner provider. You can find out if you are eligible for a loan before ever comitting to the program, so if you are interested get in touch for more information.
