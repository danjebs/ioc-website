What happens next

After we have received your enrollment we'll give you a call to confirm any details. Then we'll send you through the pre-course information with everything you need to know about Bali, visas, the course, etc. We'll also share the pre-coursework so you can start learning the basics of coding before you arrive. Our mentors will be available via email or skype before the course so we'll be ready to help you if you need a hand with anything.

You'll send us through your flight details when you have booked it, so that our driver knows exactly when to pick you up.

In the lead up to the course we'll be in close communication to help make the process as seamless as possible.
