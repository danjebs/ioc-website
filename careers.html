We're Hiring

We're trying to change the landscape of tech education, and we want you to join our team.

We're a 12 month old startup that runs coding retreats in Bali -- think coding Bootcamp + all-inclusive retreat mashed together and that's basically what we do. We take students, entrepreneurs, marketers and creatives away from the stress of day to day life, pop them down in a private pool villa, keep them nimble and relaxed with in house yoga and keep their stomachs happy with our in-house chef. Then we teach them in-demand digital skills to help them accelerate their careers. After a big day of coding, our 'extra curriculars' include things like paddle-boarding, surfing, parasailing, banana boating and just sipping cocktails and watching the sunset. Then we wake up and do it all again. It's a hard life.

Web Development Mentor

We're looking for a savvy developer to join our team as a mentor of our 10 day web development bootcamp! If waking up in a luxury pool villa in Bali or Mexico, enjoying sunrise yoga and breakfast prepared by an in-house chef before working with a team of passionate developers to teach students from all over the world how to build responsive websites from scratch sounds like your cup of tea, then read on!

We're looking for a mentor who can join us from Nov 10th - Dec 10th 2016 in Bali and ideally looking to join us next year starting in February

Skills required:

  • 2+ years experience in web development
  • strong understanding of HTML & CSS
  • moderate jQuery experience
  • excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • no teaching experience required.
  • UX or design skills appreciated but not required

On retreat you'll live in the villa with our team and 12 fantastic students -- entrepreneurs, creatives, and aspiring individuals passionate about developing their skills. We provide the curriculum, you bring your knowledge, experience and enthusiasm to the table. Ideally you will have a minimum of 3-5 years web development experience, excellent personal and communcation skills and a passion for helping others.

We're looking to fill this role as soon as possible. We will provide return flights (capped at $500), all accomodation, meals, transport, visa costs etc as well as a stipend.
