Campus Open Day

Canggu Bali | 3pm - 8pm 11th Nov 2016

COME JOIN US TO CELEBRATE OUR NEW CAMPUS IN CANGGU

To celebrate the launch of our new campus in Canggu we're hosting an open day... come and meet the team, explore our campus, and network with like minded individuals while you hang out by the pool.

Our chef will be whipping up complimentary tapas throughout the afternoon, and we'll have a bartender onsite for you to order cocktails. We'd love to meet the entrepreneurs, digital nomads and local community in Bali as well as anyone interested in attending the course who happens to be around.

ABOUT OUR 10 DAY CODING COURSE

AM

Study in
Paradise

After waking up in a tropical paradise, and maybe sneaking in a yoga session or a massage, we will dive in deep and start writing code, learning from doing... with the help of your experienced mentors, see your screen come to life from the very first day. With spectacular scenery as your backdrop, and the chef whipping up ice-cold watermelon granita to keep you refreshed, you can focus on learning, building and creating. When you need a break, take a quick dip in the pool, or sip an ice latte in the sun.

Relax &
Explore

PM

After enjoying a chef-prepared lunch in the shade, get back to it for another 1-2 hours of practical work. Then you are free to spend some one-on-one time with your experienced mentors working on your portfolio, or explore all that the town has to offer. Join us to wander through exotic markets, explore ancient rainforests, hit the surf or just relax with a cocktail by the pool. At night we'll gather back together in the villa for an indulgent 3+ course meal and some great company.
